Quick Controls Guide

  • Left click: Fire torpedo
  • Shift+click or Middle click: Fire phaser
  • Right click: Set ship heading

  • 0-9: Set warp speed (also set by clicking speed meter)
  • c: Toggle cloaking
  • s: Toggle shields
  • R: Repair mode (note this is Shift+R)
  • d: Pre-detonate nearby torpedos

  • o: Orbit planet
  • b: Bomb enemy planet
  • z: Beam up friendly armies
  • x: Beam down armies onto planet

  • For more information, see the Netrek Manual.

Game Overview

Dogfighting
  • Torpedoes and phasers use up fuel.
  • Torps do a fixed amount of damage. Phasers do more damage at close range.
  • Press d to destroy nearby torpedoes. You will take some damage, but less than a direct hit.
Planets
  • You can orbit a nearby planet by pressing o.
  • Orbiting repair planets (wrench) enhances Repair Mode.
    • Orbiting fuel planets (orange fuel can) quickly refills fuel.
  • Agri planets (blue rim) produce armies faster.
Armies
  • The goal of Netrek is to conquer planets, by moving armies from your planets onto enemy planets.
  • Orbit a firendly planet and pick up armies with z. Then, orbit an enemy planet and drop them with x. Each army you drop destroys an enemy army. When the enemy planet has no armies left, dropping another army conquers the planet for your team.
  • You can carry armies after you have made a kill.
  • Bomb an enemy planet with b to destroy armies, but bombing cannot reduce it below 4 armies.
