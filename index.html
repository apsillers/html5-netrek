Quick Controls Guide

  • Right click: Set ship heading
  • Left click: Fire torpedo
  • Middle click: Fire phaser

  • 0-9: Set warp speed (can also be set by clicking speed meter)
  • c: Toggle cloaking
  • s: Toggle shields
  • R: Repair mode (note this is Shift+R)
  • d: Pre-detonate nearby torpedos

  • o: Orbit planet
  • b: Bomb enemy planet
  • z: Beam up friendly armies
  • x: Beam down armies onto planet

Game Overview

Armies
  • The object of Netrek is to conquer the galaxy. You do this by picking up armies from your planets and dropping them on enemy planets.
  • To pick up armies, orbit a firendly planet (by pressing o) that has an army indicator and press z to pick up the armies. Then, orbit an enemy planet and drop them with x.
  • You can only carry armies after you have made a kill.
  • An planet shows an army indicator when it exceeds 4 armies.
